What is Networking?

A Network consist of two or more computers that are linked together in other to share resources(such as printer and CDs), excahange files, or allow electronic communications

In the world of computers, networking is the practice of linking two or more computing device together for the purpose of sharing data.
Networking is the proccess by which two or more computer are linked togerther for a flawless communication. By creating network, device link printers and scanners, software, and files and data that are stored in the system can be shared. It help the communication among multiple computers easy. Network are built with the mixed of computer hardware and computer software. It is the collection of the computers and other hardware components interconnected by communication channels that allow sharing of resources

Propertis of Computer Network.

  1. Facilitate communication: Computer network allow people to communicates efficiently and easily via email, instant message, chat room, telephone, video telephone calls and video confferencing.
  2. Permit sharing of files, data, and other types of information: In a network environment, authorized users may access data and information stored on other computer on the network. The capability of providing access to data and information on shared storage devices is an important feature of many networks.
  3. Shared network and computing resources: In a networked environment, each computer on a may acccess and use resources provided by device on the network, such as printing a documents on a share network printers.
  4. May be insecure: A computer network can be used by computer hackers to deploy computer viruses or computer worm on devices connected to network, or to prevent this computer from normally accessing the network
  5. May be difficult to set up: A complex computer network may be difficult to set up. It may also be costly to set up an effective computer network in large organization or company
